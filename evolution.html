The Evolution of My Craft

In high school, I enjoyed using snapchat stickers to make fun pictures.

Normally it entailed just putting new heads over other people's heads, but eventually I realized the limitations of the software.

As impressive as some of these things were that I was making, I knew I wanted to do more impressive things and grow my skills.

In November 2023, I installed the FireAlpaca software onto my PC after a friend recommended it to me.

It didn't take long for me to do my first photoshop with the software, being of some of my friends as a "family"
