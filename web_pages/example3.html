Example 3: Ice Cream with Friends

Before

After

I realized that I didn't have a good computer background of me with my high school friends, so I decided to make this.

Yeah, there's no cute story or anything with this one. I just made it because I wanted to.

This is my favorite photoshop that I have done because it was the most difficult and most satisfying one to do out of my collection.

For one, I had to fill in holes left in the background from removing the faces of the children.

I also managed to get the lighting on most of the heads to match the picture, all except for one. Sorry James.

What I impressed myself with the most, however, is that I gave the kid in the middle a completely new ice cream in his hand and it fits in seamlessly.

Overall, I really wouldn't change anything about this one. Like I said, it's my favorite creation to date.
