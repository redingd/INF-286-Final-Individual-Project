Example 1: Lunch in the Student Union

Before

After

Very rarely was my college friend group ever to all meet up in the same place at the same time due to class schedules.

Since we had never gotten more than 7 of us 8 together for a photo, I decided to take this picture of us eating lunch together in the Student Union on our campus and insert my friend who was missing here.

It may be hard to spot her, but she's center-right in the group in the after picture.

I tried my best to get the lighting on my friend consistent with the rest of us, and although I could have done better, I'm still pretty proud of it!
