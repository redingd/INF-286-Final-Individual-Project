Example 2: Polaroid in a Golf Cart

Before

After

One day when we were golfing together, my girlfriend took this picture of us together in the golf cart.

Shortly after, we had the idea to make this picture into the style of a polaroid. So, I took a stab at it.

I could definitely adjust the dimensions and the lighting to be a little more like an actual polaroid.

But, considering that I had never done something like this before, I like the way this one turned out!
